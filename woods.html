Special Properties

As I learn more about woods and some of the unique special properties that can be found, I will be sure to list them here.

A couple woods that I have worked with are:

Mahogany- a very hard wood. I have cut and cut and cut and sanded and sanded and sanded on a piece of Mahogany and seen little change.

Teak- has a high water-resistant quality.

Ebony- Dark, kinda' heavy, and will chip like a block of carbon if cut wrong.
