My wood selection

On this page I will list the woods that I have as well as the link to the online stores that I use to purchase wood. There are so many woods out there and I keep finding more!

Some people like woods for their color, some for their figure, some for their qualities. It's probably the best place to start when it comes to making decisions about a purchase.

If you have any questions, please email me. Thanks and Enjoy!

Links

Jefferies Wood Works - This is their facebook page where they show different woods they have.

Bell Forest Products - Bell has sourced some of the prettiest woods I've bought. Always good quality and they will search for woods that I can't find.

Wood Turners Catalog - This site usually caters to those turning blanks into pens and the like but they have some stock that would work for rings too. They have domestic hardwood, exotic hardwood, acrylic, and assorted other options.

Rockler - They have a selection of domestic, exotic, inlays, knife scales, and even barrel staves.

Rockler Spectraply - Now, this looks fun. This does come in a size that I can use, and I can order variety packs. They are already bundled so I don't know what colors I will get. I can order a couple at a time to try to get enough matching material.
