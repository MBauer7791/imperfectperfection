There are many ways to finish wood projects. I don't know all of the ways, but I do know a few ways.

When it comes to your PERFECT item, we will need to discuss how you want it finished.


Types of Finishes

StainCan change pale woods into just about any color, make a color richer and deeper, or make the figuring pop. (Ready Seal stains)

Polyurethane - a nature based substance that, once cured, is not harmful to the persons near it, nor the environment around it. (housefulofhandmade.com)

Wax/Polish - I enjoy using Minwax Paste Finishing Wax. It can make the figuring pop and gives solid protection.
